SERVICES

Fake news detector

We employ state-of-the-art techniques to create and test model on fake news which are generated from various sources. AI model is updated from time to time to keep it close to the latest writing trends of fake news.

Clickbait detector

Much like fake news, clickbait is a menace to the social users. With just the title of the post, our model can predict how much likely the post is a clickbait.

In-depth text analysis

An in-depth analysis of the text is performed thereby providing graphical analysis along with reading standard and difficult word count.
